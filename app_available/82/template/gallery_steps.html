${hght} Maps of candidate keypoints at each step of the algorithm.
Each circle represents a candidate keypoint. The circle radius equals 0.8 times the keypoint scale sigma.


Maps are displayed in the same order than in the SIFT algorithm:

  • A - All the DoG discrete extrema
  • B - The candidate keypoints passing the preliminary threshold on DoG (0.8 C_DoG)
  • C - The candidate keypoints after extrema interpolation
  • D - The candidate keypoints passing the threshold on DoG (C_DoG)
  • E - The candidate keypoints passing the threshold on the edge response (C_edge)
  • F - All SIFT keypoints (with their respective orientation)
  • G - The matching keypoints
