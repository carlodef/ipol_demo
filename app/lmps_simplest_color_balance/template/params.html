Algorithm Parameters

Choose the percentage of pixels saturated to 0 (s1) and 255 (s2). For example, if you choose s1=1% and s2=2% then the 1% darker pixels in each channel will be saturated to 0, and the 2% brightest pixels to 255. If the algorithm is applied on the grey level, the algorithm guarantees that none of the channels will be saturated to 0 for more than s1 of pixels nor saturated to 255 for more than s2 of pixels. Moreover, the chrominance information of the original image will be preserved (the R/G/B ratios of the original colors will be preserved).
